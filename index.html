While creating a website for VanOoteghem's Greenhouse, I went in head first never using WordPress before. Though that presented some challenges along the way, the 120+ hours I put into this project in April 2020 have been one of the greatest learning experiences of my life. I created an online store under the shop tab that included over 1,000 products, as well as an improved checkout area with the help of the WooCommerce plugin. This provided for safer purchases of plants and supplies during the global coronavirus pandemic, and they even had to end their season early because they ran out of products since the new online store helped so much. I am currently in the process of updating the site for the holiday season.

This is my extra credit generator app I submitted for the AP Computer Sciences Principles exam that I made my sophomore year in April 2018. The idea for this app came to me when my school was getting close to exams and I kept wondering how much extra credit I needed to earn the grade I desired in one of my classes. My school's grading works on a point-based system for every class, so I took some time to figure out the math to create functions that could determine how much extra credit I needed for that class, tested it multiple times, and found the generator worked for every one of my classes. I attached a slightly altered version from the one I submitted to CollegeBoard here, and also to my Instagram bio, which becomes a hit at the end of each semester.
