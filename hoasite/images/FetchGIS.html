Welcome to Bay County and Bay City FetchGIS


The Bay County and City of Bay City’s GIS programs have begun an effort over the past year to coordinate GIS projects and improve the sharing of data and resources. One of the first priorities was the creation of a GIS Web Viewer. With the teamwork and support of representatives from various county and city departments, the Bay Area Fetch Viewer was created. The Bay Area Fetch Viewer will be a valuable tool to provide better GIS services to the citizens of Bay County and Bay City as well as coordination with outside agencies in Bay County.
